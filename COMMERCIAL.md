## When you need a commercial licence

You **must** obtain a commercial licence if any of the following apply:

- You distribute a product that links to or incorporates FHE Oracle
  without releasing your product's complete source under AGPL-3.0.
- You offer a hosted service (SaaS, API, managed FHE pipeline) that
  uses FHE Oracle server-side, and you will not provide users of that
  service with the corresponding source under AGPL-3.0 (AGPL §13).
- You embed FHE Oracle in proprietary testing, compliance, or audit
  tooling that you license to customers under non-AGPL terms.
- Your legal / procurement policy prohibits AGPL software.

You do **not** need a commercial licence to:

- Evaluate FHE Oracle internally.
- Use it in academic research, publications, or teaching.
- Build an AGPL-3.0-licensed project that depends on it.
- Run it in CI/CD for an AGPL-compatible open-source project.
